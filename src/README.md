# Intro

This project encompasses several public datasets, processed to understand nonuniform coverage effects in RNA-seq.
These include:

- A mouse testis dataset that varies PCR cycle counts
- A mouse liver dataset of different studies chosen from GEO
    each with two biological replicate samples to compare across and within studies
- The SEQC UHR + HBRR datasets to determine technical effects
- A study where UHR samples were intentionally degraded to study effects of degraded
- Another study that varied PCR cycle count in UHR samples

These are written for our nonuniform coverage paper.
